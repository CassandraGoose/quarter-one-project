This website uses the IBM Watson Tone Analyzer API.

The analyzer uses linguistic analysis to detect the emotional, language style, and social tone of text. It can help people better understand the tone of their text. textSENSE uses data received from the Tone Analyzer API to show the user the emotional and social tone of the text.

The emotional tones are: Anger, Disgust, Fear, Joy, and Sadness.

The social tones are: Opennes, Conscientiousness, Extraversion, and Agreeableness.
