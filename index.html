textSENSE

textSENSE allows you to paste any English text into the textbox and receive information on the emotional tone that the text expresses. It also supplies suggestions on adjusting the emotional tone of the text and displays a visual.



When you submit your text, you will receive scores on Anger, Disgust, Fear, Joy, and Sadness. The boxes below will provide details on the emotional tone of your text.
